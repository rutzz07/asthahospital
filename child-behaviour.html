FAQ
Is aroma therapy safe for children?

Yes — we use age-appropriate, diluted blends and always check sensitivity before use.

How soon will I see change?

Parents often notice small improvements in sleep and routine within 1–3 weeks; behavioural changes build over 6–12 weeks with consistent routines.

Do you work with diagnoses like ADHD?

We provide supportive naturopathic and behavioural strategies that can complement medical care — we do not replace clinical diagnosis or specialist treatment. We coordinate with your child's healthcare team when appropriate.
